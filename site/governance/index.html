Governance

Institutional controls for publication integrity.

Immutability

Published daily outputs are treated as immutable records. Historical files are not overwritten.

Methodology Versioning

Method updates are versioned and disclosed; governance changes are documented before deployment.

Outage Handling

During provider outages or validation failures, the system withholds publication rather than emitting synthetic rates.

Change Control

Pipeline and template changes are reviewed in source control and released through auditable commits.

WITHHOLD Policy

  • Fewer than required valid sources or unacceptable data quality.
  • Dispersion above policy threshold.
  • Invalid or stale observations during the controlled window.
  • Missing critical configuration (for example, absent source credentials).
