Methodology

  • Observation window: 13:45-14:15 UTC.
  • Each source is sampled 3 times in the window.
  • Per-source value = median of that source's 3 samples.
  • Headline FIX = median of source medians.
  • BAND = 25th-75th percentile of source medians.
  • DISPERSION = (p75 - p25) / FIX.
  • Status: Green <= 1.5%, Amber <= 3.5%, Red <= 5%.
  • WITHHOLD when fewer than 2 valid sources, dispersion > 5%, or inputs are invalid/stale.
  • No backfills. Historical outputs are immutable once written.
