Methodology

Reference construction protocol and calculation standards.

  1. Observation window: 13:45-14:15 UTC.
  2. Each source is sampled three times inside the window.
  3. Per-source value equals median of that source's three samples.
  4. Headline FIX equals median of source medians.
  5. BAND equals p25-p75 across source medians.
  6. DISPERSION equals (p75 - p25) / FIX.
  7. Status bands: Green <= 1.5%, Amber <= 3.5%, Red <= 5%.
  8. WITHHOLD is triggered by invalid/stale inputs, insufficient source quality, or excessive dispersion.

Supplementary Benchmark Notes

  • NIMA Rate remains unavailable until a canonical source and symbol are explicitly approved.
  • Regional Transfer Rate is production-approved with strict canonical source mapping.
  • MOB USD Benchmark is intentionally narrower than a generic "Official Rate" label and is currently mapped to mob_usd.
